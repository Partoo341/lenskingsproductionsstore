Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Information We Collect

Lenskings Productions Store collects information you provide directly to us when you contact us via WhatsApp, phone, or email. This may include your name, phone number, email address, and communication content.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Process your orders and requests
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve our services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information about you:

We take reasonable measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights

You may request access to or deletion of your personal information by contacting us at lenskingsproductions@gmail.com or via WhatsApp at +254 704 742 748.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
